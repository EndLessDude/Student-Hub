This website makes it easy for students to track their assignments, exams, and reminders.

Tabs:
- Home
- Assignments
- Exams
- Reminders
- Calendar
- Classes

Home: 
This is a dashboard where the user can view all their assignments and exams in one place. It also has customizable "Quick Links" that cna be customized to pages such as google classroom.

Assignments:
You can add new assignments by clicking the "+ New Assignment button". You can enter the title to your assignment, enter the name of your class, add a due date, write notes, add links to pages related to the assignment. You can also view your "All" "Pending" and "Completed" assignments.

Exams: 
You can add new exams by clicking "+ New Exam". You can enter the name of your Exam, enter the name of your class, add the date of your exam, you can add objectives that you can complete to study for your exam, add links to pages related to the exam. You can also view your "All" "Pending" and "Completed" Exam.

Reminder:
You can enter the name of your remindr, add the date, add a time, customize when you want it to repeat, add notes for the reminder.

Calendar:
Here you can view your assignments, exams, and reminders in a calender view.

Classes: 
Here you can add the classes you take and add any class specific links. These will be accessible from any assignment for this class.
